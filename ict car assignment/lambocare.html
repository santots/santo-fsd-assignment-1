is an Italian manufacturer of luxury sports cars and SUVs based in Sant'Agata Bolognese.

founded Automobili Ferruccio Lamborghini S.p.A. in 1963 to compete with Ferrari.

The company was noted for using a rear mid-engine, rear-wheel drive layout.
